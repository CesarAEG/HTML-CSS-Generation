Axolotl

The axolotl, Ambystoma mexicanum, is a paedomorphic salamander related to the tiger salamander.

The species was originally found in several lakes, such as Lake Xochimilco underlying Mexico City. Axolotls are unusual among amphibians in that they reach adulthood without undergoing metamorphosis. Instead of taking to the land, adults remain aquatic and gilled.
